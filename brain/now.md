# Now

The cross-cutting current focus. Not a task list (that lives in the index). What you are actually
living in right now, across every sphere. Keep it short. Prune ruthlessly.

---

## How to use this file
Replace everything below with your real current focus. A few rules that keep it useful:
- **Rank by life, not by project.** If a relationship or your health outranks every venture this
  week, it goes at the top. The scoreboard is honest or it is useless.
- **One or two lines per item.** This is a heartbeat, not a status report. Detail lives on the card.
- **Link to cards** with `[[wikilinks]]`; do not duplicate their content here.
- **Prune on sight.** If something has not moved, it is not "now." Move it to its card or drop it.
