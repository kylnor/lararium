# Personal

Family, health, finances. The part of the system that exists so the work does not quietly eat the
life. When this sphere and a venture disagree about your week, this one is supposed to win.

## What is in here
Add only what you populate:
- `people/`: the relationships that matter.
- `health/`: the cards worth keeping (does not need an `orgs/`; never force an empty folder).
- `finances/`: the durable financial picture, not the transaction firehose.

## A note on sensitivity
This sphere holds your most private material. If you ever turn your running system into something
giftable, this is the first sphere to scrub. Keep that in mind about where you point backups and
which repos you make public.

## Current focus
The relationship or health lever that is live right now. Name the system that fixes it, not the
guilt about it.
