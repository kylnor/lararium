# Ventures

Your entrepreneurial bets and side projects. This CONTEXT.md is the front door to the sphere: what
is in it, who the recurring cast is, and what is getting hours right now.

## What is in here
- `projects/`: a card per venture. See [[example-project]] for the shape.
- `_people/`: people who span more than one venture (the `_` prefix sorts them to the top). People
  scoped to a single venture live inside that venture once it is promoted to a folder.

## The shape, applied
A venture starts as a single card in `projects/`. When it grows its own cast, its own clients, its
own sub-projects, promote it to a folder (`projects/example-project/` with its own `CONTEXT.md`,
`people/`, `reference/`). Card until it earns the folder.

## Current focus
Replace this with the one or two ventures actually getting hours. Everything else is a card you can
read when it matters, not something to track here.
